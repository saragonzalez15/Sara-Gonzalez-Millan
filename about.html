Family & Friends People I Care About

When I think of who I am, I consider the people I surround myself with. My family and friends bring out the best in me, shaping me into the person I am today. Their support and love have instilled in me the confidence to pursue my passions. They challenge me to grow and embrace new experiences, influencing my values and creating countless memories.

My family and I love to travel. I love the opportunity to experience new cultures and expose myself to new perspectives. Through traveling, I’ve learned to step out of my comfort zone, which has developed my resiliency and adaptability. I have become a more empathetic person and have learned to appreciate diversity within the world.

When I’m with my friends, I enjoy going to the beach, playing sports, and taking them to my favorite local ice cream and coffee shops. I love trying new foods, so I am always introducing my family and friends to new restaurants. When I am alone, I like to bake, cook, go to the gym, listen to music, and learn new things.
